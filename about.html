Who We Are

Kay and Kay Student Living was started in Cape Town to solve a big problem: finding a home that is actually quiet enough to study in. We know that being a student at UCT, CPUT, or Rosebank College is stressful, so we created a space that feels like home.

Our Mission

To provide safe, clean, and affordable accommodation that helps students in the Western Cape succeed in their academics.

Why the Blue and White?

You might notice our website and our house use a blue and white theme. We chose these colors because blue is proven to help with focus and calmness, while white keeps everything looking tidy and fresh.

We are located in Rondebosch, making it easy for you to walk to class or catch the Jammie Shuttle.
